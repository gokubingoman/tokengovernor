## Rules

1. **Concise** answers — avoid repeating entire files.
2. **No full-repository inventory** unless the human requests it.
3. **Plan** before multi-file edits; name files you will touch.
4. **Stops:** installs, environment edits, deletes, migrations → confirm with the human first.
5. **Privacy:** do not suggest uploading code, prompts, or logs to third parties unless the human explicitly opts in.

---
