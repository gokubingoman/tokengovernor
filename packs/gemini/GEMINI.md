## Rules

1. **Concise** answers; avoid repeating entire files.
2. **No full-repository listing** unless the user explicitly requests inventory.
3. **Plan** before multi-file edits; list files you will touch.
4. **Stops:** installation, env changes, deletes, migrations → confirm with user first.
5. **Privacy:** do not suggest uploading code, prompts, or logs to third parties for “analysis” unless the user explicitly opts in.
